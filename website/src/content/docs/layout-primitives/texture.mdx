# Texture

Applies a decorative texture behind its contents.
Use it to add dots, stripes, grids, and other patterned backgrounds without separate image assets.

## Usage

<PreviewConainer>
	<Texture texture="dots">
		<div>
			<Decorator padding={1}>
				<p>Lorem Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat. Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate velit esse cill</p>
			</Decorator>
		</div>
	</Texture>
</PreviewConainer>

<Tabs items={['HTML', 'JSX']}>
	<Tabs.Tab>
		```html
		<div data-unitone-layout="texture -texture:dots">
			<div>
				<div data-unitone-layout="decorator -padding:1">
					<p>Lorem Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. ## Props
		```

### <Badge>HTML</Badge> -texture:\{variation\} / <Badge>JSX</Badge> texture=\{variation\}

Texture type.

The available props vary depending on the type.

`dots` / `offset-dots` / `grid` / `horizontal-stripe` / `vertical-stripe` / `checker-pattern` / `graph-paper` / `slash` / `backslash` / `wave` / `solid-color` / `slash-shape`
